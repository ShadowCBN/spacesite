Who We Are

We are an organization found by the Androemeda Educational Society, whose primary aim is to produce the most developed citizens who will benefit the galaxies.

Our target is to create curiosity in the minds of students who therefore will help in creation and development of an ideal space society. We also aim to inculcate humane, compassionate and considerate values among our students.

More About Us

Our college was founded in the year 1995 on Earth, in Mumbai, India. After the unfortunate apocalypse which made Earth inhabitable, we shifted to the Androemeda Galaxy.

Space Institute of East Androemeda, previously known as South Indian Education Society's Graduate School of Technology, is one of the best institute all over Androemeda galaxy. Keeping in mind that education is the primary need for a good society and human development, we took effort to regroup and continue providing education.
