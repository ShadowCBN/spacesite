About Us

Prerana Enterprises is all about corrugation. And for us, it's never just a box. Each box we manufacture is of outstanding quality and eco-friendly!

If you are looking for custom-printed corrugated packing material, this is the precise place. We manufacture content-protecting, brand-enhancing, sustainable packaging boxes that will help your products to stand-out and get noticed thus increasing your sale. Our boxes offer sustainability and reliability helping you sell more and worry less.

Since its founding at Goregaon (Mumbai) in 1985 and later moving to Dapoli in 1998 for expansion purpose, Prerana Enterprises has developed exponentially to become a leading supplier of Corrugated Boxes (Paper Board Cartons), in Ratnagiri, Raigad, Panvel and Navi Mumbai.
